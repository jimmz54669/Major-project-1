Our Company

The Company, GDO Furniture Shop, is a medium-sized family business that was registered with the Department of Trade and Industry on July 6, 1988. In a short period of time, the company has grown significantly and has captured a market that is geared towards both in the traditional, classic and modern designed furniture. To date, Dizon Designs, the interior design arm of the company, is known for being consumer-oriented, that is reflected on its continuous efforts in offering its clientele a well-rounded approach in meeting their specific needs — from free interior design consultations to custom-made furniture to fit the client’s individual taste and lifestyle.

OUR VISION

To be considered as one of the leading and responsive furniture manufacturer in the country.

To be a service-oriented company manned by credible marketing sales people.

OUR MISSION

To set a standard in designing and developing quality furniture. To provide a selection of furniture that can very well fit the personality of clients. To be able to reach a much bigger market Worldwide.
