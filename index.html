  • Terms and Policy

    I.Price

    All prices are quoted in Philippine Pesos inclusive of VAT, delivered and installed at site within Davao City.

    II. AVAILABILITY OF STOCKS

    10 days from the date of proposal, subject to a written confirmation thereafter.

    III. PAYMENT

    The balance of the quoted and agreed price shall be paid upon completion and full delivery of the project, on fit-out projects, the balance shall be paid through progress billing.

    IV. PROGRESS BILLING

    Progress Billings will be made on a Fifteen Days (15-Days) Cycle from the commencement of works or every time the finished works becomes equal to Three Hundred Fifty Thousand Pesos (PHP 350,000.00) whichever applicable.

    V. DELIVERY

    For Standard items, delivery shall be made within a period of 10-20 working days upon receipt of purchase order, down payment, and complete specifications.

    VI. WARRANTY AND AFTER SALES SUPPORT

    A Standard One (1) Year Manufacturer’s Warranty against factory defect from date of delivery in parts and services shall apply. To ascertain the actual date of delivery, that appearing in the Delivery Receipt shall prevail.

    VII. INCLUSIONS

    Only the specific product details, layout, and drawing hardcopies are included in this proposal and warranty.

    VIII. LIMITATIONS

    Items not mentioned in the proposed deliverables, cost breakdown, and other conditions shall not form part of this proposal. Fees related to bank charges, bonds, failed pick up or delivery, re-configuration or re-consignment shall require review of the cost implication and shall necessitate a written request, prior to approval. The charges or expenses mentioned in the preceding sentence shall be considered for the account of the Client in the absence of any specific agreement between the Parties.

    IX. PENALTY

    A Penalty of One Percent (1%) monthly on all unpaid items shall be applied if Client fails to settle the obligation on the due date. For purposes of ascertaining when the due date is, it shall be the date when payment should have been made based on the completion and delivery as agreed upon by the Parties.

    X. ADDENDUM, SUPERSESSION and AMENDMENT

    Any addition, supersession and amendment to this contract after its agreement shall be stated and annexed as "ADDENDUM TO CONTRACT".

    XI. NON - DISCLOSURE

    Any information contained in the proposal shall be treated as confidential and shall not be disclosed to anyone except to the agents, employees or representatives of the Client duly authorized. Likewise, GDO furniture shall keep confidential all the specifications and details pertaining to the Client or the items purchased or ordered. Any breach of this Confidentiality Clause shall be a valid ground for the rescission of this Agreement between the Parties and may even expose the violating party to liability.
